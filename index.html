Lamborghini Revuelto

The Future of High-Performance Hybrid Supercars

Overview

The Lamborghini Revuelto is the first plug-in hybrid supercar from Lamborghini, combining a powerful V12 engine with three electric motors to deliver an astonishing 1,001 horsepower. It represents a significant step towards a more sustainable yet thrilling driving experience.

Launched in 2024, the Revuelto marks Lamborghini's commitment to innovation and environmental responsibility. This model is designed to offer the best of both worlds: the raw power and performance of a traditional supercar, coupled with the efficiency and reduced emissions of a hybrid vehicle.

Performance

With a top speed of 218 mph and the ability to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.5 seconds, the Revuelto offers unparalleled performance. The hybrid system enhances both power and efficiency, making it a true marvel of modern engineering.

The V12 engine, paired with the electric motors, provides instant torque and a seamless power delivery. The advanced all-wheel-drive system ensures optimal traction and handling, whether you're on a racetrack or navigating winding roads.

Design

The Revuelto features the iconic scissor doors and a design inspired by Lamborghini's heritage. It offers improved aerodynamics and a more spacious interior, ensuring both style and comfort.

The exterior design is characterized by sharp lines and aggressive angles, reflecting the car's high-performance capabilities. Inside, the cabin is a blend of luxury and technology, with premium materials and a driver-focused layout.

Technology

Equipped with advanced driver assistance systems and a state-of-the-art infotainment system, the Revuelto ensures a connected and safe driving experience. The hybrid technology also allows for a quieter, more efficient drive in electric-only mode.

The infotainment system includes a large touchscreen display with navigation, smartphone integration, and a premium sound system. Safety features such as adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking provide peace of mind on every journey.
