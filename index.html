New finish. Never quit.

Featuring a stunning, new black titanium case, Apple Watch Ultra 2 is the ultimate sports and adventure watch. It has all the connectivity, health and safety features for the every day. And it takes training further with the most accurate GPS in a sports watch.

Design and Build

The Apple Watch Ultra features a 49mm titanium case and a flat sapphire front crystal, offering the largest and brightest display yet. The aerospace-grade titanium provides a perfect balance of weight, durability, and corrosion resistance. The case rises to protect the edges of the flat sapphire front crystal, ensuring ruggedness and durability.

Display

The Retina display is up to 2000 nits, making it twice as bright as any previous Apple Watch. This ensures excellent visibility even in direct sunlight. The always-on display allows you to see important information at a glance without raising your wrist.

  • 3,000 nits
    LTPO2 OLED Always-On Retina display
  • 49mm
  • 410x502 pixels

Action Button

The new Action button, highlighted in international orange, is easily customizable for instant access to a variety of features, including Workouts, Compass Waypoints, Backtrack, and more. This button is designed to be noticeable in the dark and underwater, making it ideal for extreme conditions.

Battery Life

Apple Watch Ultra offers the best battery life of any Apple Watch, reaching up to 36 hours during normal use. A new low-power setting can extend battery life to up to 60 hours, making it perfect for multi-day adventures.

Health and Safety Features

Equipped with advanced health and safety features, the Apple Watch Ultra includes a blood oxygen sensor, electrical heart sensor, and third-generation optical heart sensor.

It also supports international:

  • emergency
  • calling
  • emergency SOS
  • and fall detection

GPS and Navigation

The watch includes L1 and L5 GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, QZSS, and BeiDou, providing precise location tracking. The Wayfinder watch face is designed specifically for the larger display and includes a compass built into the dial, with space for up to eight complications.

Audio and Microphones

Apple Watch Ultra features three built-in microphones to significantly improve sound quality in voice calls under any conditions. An adaptive beamforming algorithm captures your voice while reducing ambient background sounds, ensuring clear and intelligible audio even in windy environments.

Water Resistance

The watch is water-resistant up to 100 meters and is certified to EN13319, the internationally recognized standard for diving accessories. It is suitable for recreational scuba diving up to 40 meters and high-speed water sports.

Connectivity

Apple Watch Ultra supports LTE and UMTS, Wi-Fi 802.11b/g/n 2.4GHz and 5GHz, and Bluetooth 5.3. It also includes the W3 Apple wireless chip and U1 chip (Ultra Wideband) for enhanced connectivity and performance.

Environmental Features

The watch is designed with environmental sustainability in mind, using 100% recycled tungsten in the Taptic Engine, 100% recycled rare earth elements in all magnets, and 100% recycled tin in the SiP. It is also energy efficient and comes in responsible packaging.
